The Agent Ecosystem Explained

Modern AI agents are not just chatbots. They are systems that can reason, invoke tools, process context, and complete tasks across multiple steps.

What makes an agent useful

  • It can work with tools and APIs
  • It can keep context over multiple steps
  • It can make decisions and recover from mistakes

Why this matters

The best agent experiences come from combining strong models with well-designed workflows, reliable tools, and clear guardrails.
