Before You Write

  • Corrections: If you spot outdated pricing or incorrect model data, please include a link to the official source so we can verify and update quickly.
  • Model additions: We're happy to consider adding new models to our comparison tables. Please provide the model name, provider, context window, pricing, and links to official documentation.
  • Guest content: We occasionally publish guest articles from developers and industry experts. If you'd like to contribute, send a brief pitch outlining your topic and why it would be valuable to our readers.
  • Link removal: We link to official provider websites as a convenience to readers. If you believe a link on our site is inappropriate, please let us know.

Our Commitment

We read every message we receive. While we may not be able to respond to every inquiry individually, we take all feedback seriously and use it to improve the site. Corrections to factual errors are prioritized and typically addressed within 48 hours.
